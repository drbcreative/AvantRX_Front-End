BOTOX

Botox, a wrinkle relaxer, is used to prevent, soften and weaken the repetitive muscle movement that causes lines along the forehead, around the eyes, and those “11s” between the eyebrows. In relaxing these expressive muscles and smoothing out the creases of the skin, you will look more refreshed and rested in just one treatment.

FILLERS

Defying the Law of Gravity, dermal fillers will refresh a sagging or sunken face to refresh your appearance. Made of hyaluronic acid, fillers restore facial volume, contour the face for symmetry, and plump the skin due to loss of collagen, elastin and fat.
